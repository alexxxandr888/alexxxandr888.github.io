About the author

Ismail Useinov

I have been involved in web development since 2013.

In 2017, he founded the ITDoctor training YouTube channel. I share my knowledge in the field of programming and website creation. He has formed his own teaching methodology, which allows students to master the material being studied as effectively as possible.

I teach HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, working with preprocessors, assemblers and the Git version control system, as well as much more from the world of web technologies. More than a thousand of my students have already learned how to create websites on their own and make money from it.

In this video course I want to teach you the basics of website building - the languages HTML and CSS. These languages are the basis and everyone who wants to develop in this area should know them. You will gain the knowledge that is needed at the very beginning of the journey of any web developer and will use it throughout your entire journey. All theoretical information is neatly explained, clearly explained and supported by practical assignments and homework. The course deliberately does not contain unnecessary information, thereby allowing you to better master the material that is really important.
